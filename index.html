Biography

I’m Caroline Ceribeli, a Ph.d. student in Chemistry at the Institute of Chemistry of São Carlos of the University of São Paulo. During my MSc in Chemistry, I mainly worked on the following topics: analytical chemistry, food chemistry, meat, redox stability, NMR, and EPR. I’m graduated in Exact Sciences with a specialization in Physics (in 2013) and a specialization in Chemistry (in 2012).
